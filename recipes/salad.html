Caesar Salad

Description

Caesar Salad is a very basic, but often overlooked salad. With a little work it can be made into a delicious addition to any meal.

Ingredients

  • Head of Lettuce
  • 1 Avocado
  • 1 Bell Pepper of any color
  • Croutons
  • String Cheese
  • 1 Tomato
  • Baby Carrots
  • Dressing of Choice
