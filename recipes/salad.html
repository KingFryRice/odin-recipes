Caesar Salad

Description

Caesar Salad is a very basic, but often overlooked salad. With a little work it can be made into a delicious addition to any meal.

Ingredients

  • Head of Lettuce
  • 1 Avocado
  • 1 Bell Pepper of any color
  • Croutons
  • String Cheese
  • 1 Tomato
  • Baby Carrots
  • Dressing of Choice

Steps

  1. Wash lettuce and chop into bite sized pieces
  2. Place chopped lettuce in large bowl to mix rest of ingredients in
  3. Mix the following items into the bowl with the lettuce:
    • Sliced avocado
    • Diced bell pepper
    • Cubed string cheese
    • Diced tomato
    • Diced baby carrots
    • Croutons
  4. Drizzle dressing into bowl and mix until everything is coated in the bowl
  5. Serve and enjoy!
