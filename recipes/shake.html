Mint Chip Shake

Description

A mint chip shake is the perfect dessert to finish out a long day. With endless possibilities, you can upgrade your plain mint chip ice cream to a fine delicacy!

Ingredients

  • Blender
  • Tub of mint chip ice cream
  • Milk
  • Optional:
    • Oreos
    • Whip Cream
    • Chocolate sauce
    • 1 Cherry

Steps

  1. In a blender place 4 scoops of ice cream
  2. Pour milk into blend until it appears the ice cream is half covered
  3. Blend mixture until it is a consistency you prefer (more milk for a more liquid shake)
  4. Pour shake into a cup
  5. If desired, added crushed oreos, whip cream, chocolate sauce, and cherry
  6. Enjoy!
