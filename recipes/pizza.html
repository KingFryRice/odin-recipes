Margherita Pizza

Description

Margherita Pizza is a simple, but delicious pizza that is extremely easy to make and enjoy at home. It's perfect for first time pizza makers at home, or seasoned professionals!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
    • Store bought may be easy, but it is not too hard to make your own dough if you're feeling adventurous!
  • Flour
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Fresh Basil leaves
  • Cornmeal
  • Pizza Stone

Steps

  1. Start by preheating your oven to 400 o
  2. While the oven is heating up, sprinkle your work area with a healthy amount of flour
  3. Take your dough and lay it onto your floury work space
  4. Begin to kneed and stretch the dough out with your hands
  5. Once you are happy with the shape of your dough, spread cornmeal onto the pan you will use to transfer the dough into the oven
  6. Lay your dough onto the pan and get your pizza sauce ready
  7. With a spoon, place as much sauce onto your pizza as you wish
  8. Finally, dollop your mozzarella cheese onto several places across the pizza
  9. When the oven is ready, place your pizza in the oven
  10. Watch pizza carefully and cook for 10 minutes, rotating as needed to cook evenly
  11. When done, take your pizza out, garnish with fresh basil, cut and serve!
